Colors ðŸ”—

All the colors used on the Geizhals Preisvergleich website. We aim to reduce the amount of different colors in use and stick to the colors defined in this document.

Colors are grouped into a few categories:

  • Colors
    • Geizhals Brand Color
    • Accent colors
    • Grey variations
    • Offer button colors
    • Offer availability colors
    • Additional colors
Please make sure you use colors in an accessible manner. For example, use white text on red-base or green-base background, but black text on apricot-base.
